# Design System: vagas.rankia.cloud

## Direction
Personality: Precision & Density
Foundation: Cool Slate (`slate-950` base background)
Depth: Borders-only + Glassmorphism

## Tokens
### Spacing
Base: 4px
Scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48

### Colors
- **Canvas Base**: `#09090b` (Slate-950)
- **Surface Level 1 (Cards/Form)**: `rgba(24, 24, 27, 0.45)` (Slate-900 border-coated)
- **Surface Level 2 (Dropdowns/Floating)**: `rgba(39, 39, 42, 0.95)`
- **Brand Accent**: `#2563eb` (Tech Blue) -> `#3b82f6` (Light Tech Blue)
- **Semantic Destructive/Spam**: `#ef4444` (Ruby Crimson)
- **Semantic Success/EasyApply**: `#10b981` (Emerald Green)
- **Text Primary**: `#f8fafc` (Slate-50)
- **Text Secondary**: `#cbd5e1` (Slate-300)
- **Text Muted**: `#64748b` (Slate-500)

### Border Radius
- Small (Buttons, Controls): 10px
- Medium (Cards, Forms): 16px
- Large (Modals, Popovers): 24px

## Patterns
### Button Primary
- Height: 44px
- Padding: 12px 24px
- Radius: 12px
- Background: Gradient from Tech Blue to Light Tech Blue

### Card Result
- Border: `0.5px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`
- Background: `rgba(24, 24, 27, 0.45)`
- Radius: 16px
- Padding: 24px
